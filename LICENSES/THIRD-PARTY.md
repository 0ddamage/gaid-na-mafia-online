Third-Party Components
======================

This distribution includes bundled third-party bytecode tooling within
`_core/bin/repackgender-core.jar`.

Included component:

- ASM
  - upstream project: https://asm.ow2.io/
  - license: BSD-3-Clause
  - bundled namespaces:
    - `org/objectweb/asm`
    - `org/objectweb/asm/signature`
    - `org/objectweb/asm/tree`

The applicable license text for the bundled ASM component is provided in
`LICENSES/ASM-BSD-3-Clause.txt`.
